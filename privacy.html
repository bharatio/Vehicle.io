Privacy & Responsible Use

Bharat.io is built with privacy, safety, and responsible communication as its core principles. This page explains how information is used and what is expected from users of the Bharat.io QR system.

Information Collection

Vehicle owners voluntarily provide their contact details during registration. This may include a mobile number, email address, vehicle number, and optional professional or business information.

Bharat.io does not require users to install any mobile application, create accounts, or share unnecessary personal data beyond what is required for parking or emergency contact.

How Information Is Used

Contact information is displayed only when a specific QR sticker is scanned. The details are shown solely to enable direct communication between the person scanning the QR and the vehicle owner.

Bharat.io does not sell, rent, or use personal data for advertising, marketing campaigns, or promotional activities.

Public Visibility & Consent

Vehicle owners explicitly consent to share their contact details for parking assistance and emergency situations only. Owners may choose to include optional professional information to help identify themselves without revealing unnecessary personal details.

Responsible Use Policy

The Bharat.io system must not be used for spam, harassment, promotional calls, or any non-essential communication. Misuse of the system goes against its intended purpose and community guidelines.

Bharat.io reserves the right to restrict access or disable QR codes in cases of repeated misuse or abuse.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information. However, Bharat.io operates as a simple connectivity platform and does not guarantee uninterrupted or error-free service in all situations.

Updates to This Policy

This privacy and responsible use policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements or regulatory requirements. Continued use of the Bharat.io system implies acceptance of the latest version of this page.

For questions or concerns related to privacy, please contact us at: support@bharat.io
